About Us

Who We Are

Welcome to Chemical Factory, a leader in the field of chemical manufacturing, research, and development. Established in 2024, we have grown to become a trusted partner for industries worldwide, renowned for our innovative solutions, commitment to sustainability, and unwavering dedication to quality.

What We Do

At Chemical Factory, we specialize in the production and development of a wide range of chemical products used across various industries, including pharmaceuticals, agriculture, manufacturing, and consumer goods. Our extensive portfolio includes:

  • Specialty Chemicals: Custom-formulated chemicals tailored to specific industrial needs.
  • Advanced Materials: Innovative compounds that enhance product performance and sustainability.
  • Green Chemistry: Eco-friendly alternatives that reduce environmental impact.

Why Choose Us

Infrastructure

We operate three state-of-the-art manufacturing facilities in India, strategically located near consumption centers and raw material sources.

Sustainability

In addition to our in-house R&D, KCI partners with academia and industry to ensure that growth and sustainability go hand-in-hand. Our efforts in this domain have been recognized with several awards.

Social Responsibility

As we grow, we ensure we give back to the community. Our primary focus is on education and skill development, particularly for women.

Corporate Group

KCI is part of a diversified, well-established corporate family. We are a values-driven group, emphasizing quality, partnerships, and social responsibility.
